Who I Am

A bit
about me

I'm Nirmal — a multidisciplinary designer and front-end developer based in Dublin. I've spent the last six years helping startups, studios, and scale-ups build products people actually want to use.

My approach blends rigorous systems thinking with an obsessive eye for visual craft. I'm equally at home in a Figma file and a code editor, and I believe the best work happens when those two worlds collapse into one.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me writing about craft, hiking, or hunting for the world's best flat white.
